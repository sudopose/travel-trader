# Travel Trader 2.0 - Comprehensive Overhaul Plan

## 1. NEW GAMEPLAY MECHANICS

### Dynamic Seasons System
- **Spring:** All food prices +20%, spices cheaper
- **Summer:** Luxury goods +30%, travel costs +10%
- **Autumn:** Gold prices +25%, materials +15%
- **Winter:** All prices +10%, travel costs +20%

### Weather Events
- **Storms:** +50% travel costs, goods spoil chance
- **Droughts:** Food prices +40%, spices skyrocket
- **Fog:** Reduced visibility (can't see distant cities)
- **Bloom:** All flowers and crops temporary bonuses

### Market Volatility Meter
- Visual gauge showing current market stability
- High volatility = massive price swings, rare events
- Low volatility = steady prices, better for safe trading
