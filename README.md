## How to Play

### Objective
Build your fortune by trading exotic goods across the world. Buy low, sell high, and unlock new destinations.

### Getting Started
1. **Start:** Begin in Istanbul or Mumbai with 200 gold coins.
2. **Trade:** Check market prices in cities like Istanbul, Mumbai, and Venice.
3. **Buy:** Purchase goods (Silk, Gold, Spices) when prices are low (green indicators).
4. **Travel:** Choose your next destination. Watch out for travel costs!
5. **Sell:** Sell goods for profit in new cities.
6. **Unlock:** Save up to unlock new luxury destinations like Beijing and New York.

### Tips for Success
- **Consult Together:** Look at market prices together in your current city.
- **Strategy:** Decide which goods are likely to bring the most profit.
- **Watch Events:** Keep an eye on market events like "Gold Rushes" or "Shortages" that dramatically affect prices.
- **Plan Routes:** Some cities are cheaper to travel to than others. Plan your loop efficiently.

### Game Goods
- **Food:** Wheat, Rice, Tea, Coffee
- **Spices:** Spices
- **Luxury:** Silk, Gold, Diamonds, Ivory
- **Materials:** Cotton

### Locations
- **Istanbul** - Starting hub, crossroads of trade.
- **Mumbai** - Spices and tea paradise.
- **Cairo** - Gold and ivory markets.
- **Venice** - Luxury goods destination.
- **Beijing** - Unlock for 500 coins.
- **New York** - Unlock for 1000 coins.

### Events
- **Gold Rushes** - Gold prices skyrocket! Buy and sell immediately.
- **Shortages** - Goods become scarce and expensive.
